NEON DRIFT

RACING

INSTRUCTIONS

Single Player Controls

  • W - Accelerate
  • S - Brake
  • A - Turn Left
  • D - Turn Right

Two Player Controls

Player 1 (Cyan): W/S/A/D

Player 2 (Magenta): ↑/↓/←/→

Gameplay

Single Player

  • Pass through all checkpoints to complete a lap.
  • Try to beat your best lap time!

Two Players

  • Within 30s, try to reach more checkpoints than your opponent.
  • Used checkpoints will reactivate after a time period.
  • Whoever gets to more checkpoints wins!

Other Controls

  • E - Toggle Particle Effects
  • R / Right Control - Respawn
  • ESC - Pause Menu

PAUSED

GAME OVER

Player 1 (Cyan): 0 checkpoints
Player 2 (Magenta): 0 checkpoints
Press R to Restart
Press M for Main Menu
