NEON DRIFT

RACING

INSTRUCTIONS

Single Player Controls

  • W / ↑ - Accelerate
  • S / ↓ - Brake / Reverse
  • A / ← - Turn Left
  • D / → - Turn Right
  • Space - Handbrake (Force Drift)

Two Player Controls

Player 1 (Cyan): W/A/S/D + Shift

Player 2 (Magenta): Arrow Keys + Space

Gameplay

  • Pass through all checkpoints to complete a lap
  • Drift at high speed to earn drift points
  • Chain drifts together for combo multipliers
  • Wall hits reset your drift combo
  • Try to beat your best lap time!

Other Controls

  • P - Pause / Resume
  • R - Reset Game
  • ESC - Return to Menu
